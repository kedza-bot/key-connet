About Us

My web app helps people connect for learning, sharing useful information, posting jobs, and most importantly — connecting with others.

The problem I’m solving is that it’s hard for junior developers like me to connect with senior developers. With this app, I make it possible for dreamers to access free resources and opportunities to become whatever they want to be — all without subscription fees.

Using blogs within the app, anyone can share knowledge, learn new skills, and inspire others. My mission is to create a better Africa for Africans — showing that we can build and use technology just as effectively as anywhere else in the world.

I’ve already built an MVP that’s live and running, and I’m proud to say I entered the FNB App Academy competition using the skills I gained from the academy. Shoutout to IT Varsity and Skillseeker Hub — a must-visit place for web developers on TikTok.

Our Aim

To bridge the gap between aspiring developers and experienced professionals, fostering a culture of free learning, sharing, and collaboration.

Our Motto

LOTS — Listen more, Observe, Try it, Simplify it.
